Salata de Vinete

Also known as Romanian eggplant dip

Salata de vinete is a traditional food made in romania. If you have not hear of vinete, then you might of heard of Baba Ghanoush. They are very similar in taste but Vinete does not have spice. Vinete is a healthy spread that can be eaten with bread, crackers and vegetables. For the people that dont like eggplant, dont worry you cant even taste it! I would know, im one of those people.

Prep time - 5 minutes

Cook time - 50 minutes

Additional time - 15 minutes

Total time - 1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 eggplants

  • 1 green onion - diced

  • 1 clove garlic

  • Vegetable oil

  • Lemon juice

  • Salt

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 200Â°c.

    2. Poke holes in the eggplant skin to cook the inside.

    3. Put eggplant on baking sheet or spray with non-stick spray.

    4. Cook eggplant for 50 minutes, turning it a few times for an evenly cooked plant.

    5. When the eggplant is squishy and soft (the skin should be black) take it out the oven and let cool.

    6. When fully cooled, remove the eggplants skin.

    7. Place eggplant in cheese cloth and squeeze out all the liquid from inside.

    8. Put the eggplant in a food processor and season with salt.

    9. Add lemon juice and the diced green onion.

    10. Start blening until a nice half chunky,partly liquidy consistancy is formed.

    11. Put in bowl and add some salt and green onion for extra flavour and looks.
